Cookies Policy

Effective Date: 14/04/2024

At Aetheris Studio, we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience on our website. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

‍1. What Are Cookies?

‍Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information and help personalize the user experience.

‍2. How We Use Cookies

‍We use cookies for a variety of reasons detailed below. In general, these cookies help us:

- Ensure the website functions properly
- Enhance the performance of the site by analyzing user behavior
- Remember your preferences to personalize content
- Provide targeted advertising and marketing communications

The types of cookies we use include:
‍
‍a. Necessary Cookies
‍These cookies are essential for the website to function correctly and cannot be disabled in our systems. They allow basic functionalities such as page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.

‍b. Performance Cookies
‍These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. We use this data to improve the performance and functionality of the site.

‍c. Functional Cookies
‍These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make, such as language preferences or login information. This helps us tailor the website to your needs.

‍d. Targeting/Advertising Cookies
‍These cookies track your browsing habits so that we can show you relevant ads based on your interests. They are also used to limit the number of times you see a particular ad and measure the effectiveness of ad campaigns.

‍3. Third-Party Cookies

‍In addition to our own cookies, we may use third-party cookies, such as those from Google Analytics, Facebook, or other marketing platforms. These third parties may use the information collected through their cookies to provide you with personalized content and ads on other websites.

‍4. Managing Cookies

‍You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can manage your cookie preferences in the following ways:

- Browser Settings: Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies or alert you when a cookie is being set. For detailed instructions, consult your browser’s help section.
- Opt-out Tools: Some third-party services provide tools to opt-out of data collection, such as Google Analytics’ opt-out browser add-on or Facebook’s ad preferences settings.
- Cookie Preferences: You can use the Cookie preferences control on this website to accept or reject Google Analytics and similar non-essential measurement scripts.

Please note that blocking or disabling cookies may affect your ability to use some parts of our website and could impair the functionality of certain features.

‍5. Changes to This Policy

‍We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the technologies we use or for legal or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information on our use of cookies.
