Information

What the site and qualification form collect

Information you submit

The commerce qualification form asks for information needed to understand the business and the proposed work:

  • your name, work email address, role, company or brand name and store or company URL;
  • commerce platform, annual revenue band, monthly advertising-spend band and primary market;
  • relevant workstreams, the commercial problem, the event that triggered the enquiry and the desired timeline;
  • project-budget band, decision-making or sponsor readiness, and any context entered in the optional constraint field;
  • the privacy-notice version, acceptance time and your separate choice about optional marketing contact.

Attribution and technical context

When the qualification experience loads, the site records a first-touch and current-touch attribution snapshot. This may include UTM campaign values, advertising click identifiers such as GCLID, WBRAID or GBRAID, a sanitised landing path, a sanitised referrer and capture time. Arbitrary query-string values and URL fragments are removed from stored landing and referrer URLs.

The first-touch snapshot is held in your browser's local storage under aetheris_studio_attribution_v1 and is included with the form only if you send it. The form is not dependent on this storage. See the cookies and browser storage notice for controls.

Cloudflare Pages and Cloudflare Turnstile may process request metadata, device or browser signals and an IP address to deliver the site and protect the form from abuse. The server forwards the connecting IP to Turnstile's verification service when it is available; the qualification ledger sent to Attio does not include that IP address.

Triage

How qualification and scoring work

Aetheris uses a deterministic, rules-based score to triage website requests. The rules consider commerce readiness, primary market, scale indicators, the clarity of the problem, timing, stated project budget and access to a decision maker or sponsor. The score assigns an internal fit and priority level.

A high-fit result can reveal the option to continue to Aetheris Studio's Cal.com booking page. Other valid requests enter a review outcome and may be assessed manually. The public response is deliberately limited to qualified or review; it does not expose the score or internal reasons.

This triage does not accept or reject a contract and is not intended to produce a legal or similarly significant effect. Aetheris can review the underlying request and decide how to respond. If you want a person to reconsider the route applied to your request, contact us using the details above.

The scoring explanation and its legal characterization must be confirmed during the required pre-production legal review.

Use

Why we use this information

  • Assess and answer an enquiry: understand the request, determine likely mutual fit, contact you and take steps you request before a possible engagement.
  • Operate the lead workflow: create or update the relevant person and company in Aetheris's CRM, keep the inbound request with its qualification context and coordinate follow-up.
  • Protect the form and service: verify Turnstile tokens, validate request origins and payloads, prevent automated abuse and maintain service security.
  • Understand acquisition context: associate submitted enquiries with the campaign or source that brought the browser to the site.
  • Optional marketing: send marketing communication only where you made the separate optional choice to receive it, subject to the final approved consent language and applicable law.
  • Meet legal obligations and protect rights: keep records where required and establish, exercise or defend legal claims.

The production notice must identify and validate the applicable legal basis for each purpose under the law that applies to the final service. Optional marketing consent is separate from the permission to contact you about the request and may be withdrawn without affecting the original enquiry.

Recipients

Where the information goes

Attio CRM

After validation and Turnstile verification, the server uses Attio as Aetheris's customer relationship management platform. It upserts the company by store domain and the person by work email, records the Studio business unit and an internal priority, appends a website-inbound qualification ledger, and adds the person to the dedicated Website Inbound list.

Cloudflare

Cloudflare provides Pages hosting, edge request handling and Turnstile abuse protection. The browser loads the Turnstile widget from Cloudflare and the server validates its short-lived token with Cloudflare before any CRM write.

Cal.com and other disclosures

The qualification form does not automatically send its contents to Cal.com. If a qualifying visitor chooses the booking link, the browser opens Cal.com and any information entered there is handled under that service's notice. Aetheris may also disclose information to professional advisers, competent authorities or other providers when necessary for the purposes above and permitted by law.

Some providers may process information outside the European Economic Area. The provider roles, processing terms, locations and transfer safeguards must be verified and described with legal counsel before this draft is approved for production.

Stewardship

Retention and security

Aetheris will keep enquiry and CRM information only for as long as needed for the lead or client relationship, related operational needs, applicable legal obligations and the establishment, exercise or defence of claims. Optional marketing information should no longer be used for that purpose after a valid withdrawal, subject to any minimal suppression record required to honour it.

This draft intentionally does not invent a fixed retention period. The final retention schedule and deletion criteria must be defined and legally reviewed before production launch.

The implementation uses server-side credentials, encrypted hosting secrets, same-origin and hostname checks, payload limits, a honeypot, Turnstile verification and restricted CRM fields. No internet transmission or storage system is completely risk-free, and these measures do not constitute a guarantee of absolute security.

Your choices

Your data-protection rights

Depending on the applicable law and the circumstances, you may ask Aetheris to:

  • confirm whether your personal data is processed and provide access to it;
  • correct inaccurate or incomplete information;
  • erase information or restrict its use;
  • provide portable information where the right applies;
  • object to processing based on legitimate interests or to direct marketing;
  • withdraw consent for future processing where consent is the basis; and
  • obtain human review of the qualification route applied to your request.

You may exercise a request by emailing info@aetherisstudio.com or the certified email address above. Aetheris may need to verify your identity. You may also complain to the competent data-protection supervisory authority. Withdrawing consent does not affect processing already carried out lawfully before withdrawal.
